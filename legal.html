AI Disclaimer and Policy

Last updated: January 2025 | Version 1.0

⚠ Important: Read Before Using the Service

Operion uses artificial intelligence to generate responses to your customers' enquiries. This means automated responses are sent in your name without human review of each individual message. You, as the business owner and account holder, are legally responsible for all communications sent from your email address, including those generated by Operion.

How Our AI Works

Operion uses three locally-hosted large language models: Llama2 (primary), Mistral (secondary fallback), and Phi (tertiary fallback). These models run on our own infrastructure — no data is sent to OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, or any other external AI provider. If all three models are unavailable, a static template response is used to ensure customers always receive a reply.

What the AI Uses to Generate Responses

  • Your business profile (name, brand voice, services, operating hours)
  • Your knowledge base (FAQs and custom entries you provide)
  • The customer's message
  • Classification of the enquiry type

The AI does not have access to the internet, your email history, or any data outside of what you have provided to Operion and what the customer sent in their message.

AI Limitations and Risks

You must be aware of and accept the following limitations:

  • AI can make mistakes. Responses may occasionally be inaccurate, inappropriately worded, or fail to address the customer's actual need correctly.
  • Context is limited. The AI responds based only on information in your profile and knowledge base. Missing information leads to generic responses.
  • AI cannot exercise judgement. Complex situations requiring nuanced human judgement may not be handled appropriately by AI.
  • Static fallback. When all AI models are unavailable, a pre-written template is sent. This is clearly disclosed to you via alerts but is not disclosed to your customers.
  • No guarantee of accuracy. Operion does not guarantee that AI-generated responses will be accurate, complete, legally compliant, or appropriate for your specific business context.

Your Responsibilities

  • Maintain an accurate and complete business profile and knowledge base
  • Regularly review your HIVE Mind intelligence reports and dashboard
  • Configure appropriate escalation rules for sensitive enquiry types
  • Inform your customers that AI may be used to handle their enquiries (we recommend adding a note to your privacy policy)
  • Not use Operion for enquiries where AI errors could cause safety risks, medical risks, legal liability, or financial harm
  • Monitor for and report any AI responses that breach legal or ethical standards

Prohibited Uses of AI Features

You must not use Operion to handle:

  • Medical diagnoses or clinical advice
  • Legal advice that creates a solicitor-client relationship
  • Financial advice regulated under FCA rules
  • Emergency services communications (999/112 calls or similar)
  • Safeguarding or child protection communications
  • Any communication where an AI error could endanger a person's life, health, or safety

HIVE Mind and Automated Learning

Our HIVE Mind system analyses your enquiry patterns and response quality, and may propose improvements to your response templates and knowledge base. No HIVE Mind improvements are automatically applied. All proposed changes are held in a pending queue and require your explicit approval before activation. You can review and manage all HIVE recommendations via your Admin Portal.

Copyright and AI-Generated Content

AI-generated responses created by Operion are produced on your behalf and within your business context. You own the responses sent from your account. However, given the nature of AI-generated content, we make no claim of copyright on behalf of Operion in respect of these responses, and disclaim all warranties regarding their originality or intellectual property status.
