How It Works

Three layers of intelligence. One outcome: every customer answered.

Operion is not software you install. It is a managed service that runs on our infrastructure. You tell us about your business. We handle everything else.

Architecture

Three concentric layers

Outer Layer — Expansion

Operion Ecosystem

The outer wrapper. Connects the platform to the world. Ingests external knowledge from industry sources. Hosts the Collective Intelligence Network — anonymised patterns aggregated across all businesses. Runs the Automation Marketplace. Monitors system health every 5 minutes.

Middle Layer — Intelligence

Operion HIVE Mind

The brain. Completely async — never interferes with enquiry processing. Runs every 10 minutes. Detects 7 pattern types, scores every delivered response, identifies KB gaps, proposes improvements. All changes require your approval before activation.

Core — Execution Engine

Operion Core

The gateway. Every enquiry enters here. Every response exits here. Contains the Orchestrator, GUARDIAN security, Intelligence Engine, Response Engine, Stripe payment management, self-test suite, dashboard API, and onboarding system. The thing that does the actual work.

The Engines

What's inside Operion Core

🔀 Orchestrator Engine

Central coordination. Every enquiry from every channel is normalised to a standard event schema before anything else happens.

🛡 GUARDIAN Security Engine

SQL injection, XSS, prompt injection, spam, phishing — blocked before they reach your AI. Threats are logged and you are alerted.

🧠 Intelligence Engine

Intent classification (15 types), entity extraction (dates, amounts, references, urgency), priority scoring, vector knowledge search.

⚡ Response Engine

Llama2 → Mistral → Phi → Static fallback. Four-tier failsafe. Your customers always receive a response, regardless of AI availability.

📬 Queue Engine

Priority queue, deduplication, automatic retry, dead-letter handling. Designed to never drop an enquiry even under load.

💳 Stripe Payment Engine

Automatic tier activation, subscription management, payment confirmation, failure alerts. Full audit trail. No card data stored.

The AI

Locally hosted. No external AI APIs.

All AI processing runs on our own hardware using Ollama-hosted models. No data is sent to OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, or any external AI provider. Your enquiries and your customers' data stay within our infrastructure.

This also means there are no per-token costs — your monthly subscription covers all AI processing, regardless of enquiry volume.

AI failsafe chain
1 Llama2 (primary) → fast, capable
2 Mistral (fallback) → if Llama2 fails
3 Phi (knowledge) → if Mistral fails
4 Static template → always works
✓ Customer always gets a response
✓ You're alerted if fallback is used
✓ No external API. Your data stays local.
