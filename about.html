About

We built the engine.
You keep the relationship.

Operion started from a simple observation: every small business in the world has the same problem. Customers send messages. Someone has to read every one, work out what they need, and write back. That takes time, costs money, and stops the moment the owner goes on holiday or gets sick.

We built Operion to solve that problem completely — not partially, not with a chatbot that needs constant supervision, but autonomously. A system that receives every enquiry, understands it, responds in the business's own voice, and gets smarter with every interaction.

It runs on our infrastructure. The business owner pays a monthly subscription and never thinks about it again. Their customers just get answered.

What we believe

Honesty about AI

AI is genuinely useful and genuinely imperfect. We never pretend otherwise. Our AI Disclaimer is prominent, our terms are plain English, and we require every client to confirm they've read and understood what they're agreeing to. We think that's the minimum standard for deploying AI in a commercial context.

Human oversight built in

HIVE Mind can learn, spot patterns, and propose improvements — but nothing changes in your system without your explicit approval. We built the admin approval gate before we built anything else. Automation without oversight is not a product, it's a liability.

Your data is yours

We don't use your customer data to train models. We don't share it with third parties. We don't sell it. Our AI runs on our own hardware so no external provider ever sees your enquiries. This isn't just policy — it's architecture.

Small businesses deserve enterprise-grade tools

A plumber in Manchester shouldn't need a £200k software budget to give their customers fast, intelligent responses. Operion gives any business — any size, any sector — the same quality of AI that enterprise companies pay fortunes for, at a price that makes sense for a small business.
