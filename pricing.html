Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Are there any hidden fees?

No. Your monthly subscription covers everything. No per-enquiry charges, no API costs, no overage fees. Enquiries above your hourly limit are rate-limited, not charged extra.

Can I change tier at any time?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. All tier changes are logged in your audit trail.

What happens if I cancel?

Your service continues until the end of your paid period. After that, your account is suspended. Your data is retained for 30 days before deletion. You can reactivate at any time.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. You fill in an onboarding form. We do everything else. The only technical requirement is knowing your email server settings — most email providers make this very easy to find.

Is my data secure?

All data is processed and stored within the UK. We never sell your data. We don't use external AI providers — all processing is done on our own hardware. See our Privacy Policy.

What is the AI Disclaimer about?

Operion uses AI to generate responses. You remain legally responsible for what is sent from your account. We are transparent about this. Read our full AI Policy before subscribing.

Ready to stop missing enquiries?

Your first 30 days are covered by our money-back guarantee.
