How It Works

From enquiry to response in seconds

Operion runs entirely on our infrastructure. Your customers contact you as they always have. We handle everything in between.

01

Customer sends an enquiry

By email, contact form, or any other channel. It arrives at Operion automatically.

02

Operion reads and understands it

Our AI classifies the enquiry, extracts key details, and searches your knowledge base for relevant context.

03

A response is generated in your voice

Using your brand voice, your services, and your specific knowledge — not a generic template.

04

Sent from your email address

Your customer receives a reply from you. You receive a weekly report on everything that was handled. We recommend notifying customers in your privacy policy that AI may be used to handle their enquiries.

AI Transparency Notice

Operion uses locally-hosted AI models (Llama2, Mistral, Phi) to generate responses. All AI output is reviewed against your business context and knowledge base. Responses are sent in your business name and you remain responsible for the content delivered to your customers. See our AI Policy for full details.
